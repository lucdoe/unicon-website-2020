thanks for being part of unicon 2019

100 participants, 5 speeches, 6 workshops, 1 challenge PLUS afterparty

Join our digital product conference that is made by students and aims to connect students from inspiring universities and diverse backgrounds through tech.

Speeches

We invite renowned speakers to talk about their personal experiences with developing digital products. Last year´s speakers included CXOs from Trivago, Project A Ventures, Outfittery and Blinkist. For unicon 2020, we have convinced amazing entrepreneurs to join us.

The Challenge

In oder to combine the diverse skills of our attendees, we will organize a group challenge in which they collaborate for four hours in small teams to come up with a concept of a technical solution for a specific problem. Each group consists of software engineers, product managers and interaction designers.

Workshops

To enable close contact to industry leaders with high credibility and many years of experience as well as hands on deep dives, we will organise several masterclasses and workshops about specific topics regarding digital product development.

Former Speakers

We are not only looking at the companies they have founded or worked for, but also what they have experienced on a personal level and what of those experience can support us - as students - most.
